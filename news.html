OUR WORK

WE ENVISION A WORLD WHERE COMMUNITIES THRIVE WITH NATURE ACROSS GENERATIONS.

Youth4Nature is a youth-led, international non-profit organization that educates, empowers, and mobilises young people to lead on solutions for the ecological and climate crises that are rooted in science, aligned with traditional, local and Indigenous knowledge, and grounded in climate and intergenerational justice. Our mission is three-fold:
  • To mobilise decision-makers to take ambitious action for nature and climate by 2030 that creates co-benefits for social and human well-being;
  • To elevate the voices of youth by providing a platform to share their stories and have them be heard; and,
  • To bridge youth action between the biodiversity and climate movements, while building their capacity as stewards for nature and climate.

KNOWLEDGE-SHARING

We connect young people with scientists, experts, and knowledge-holders to build a strong knowledge-base among young people globally. Our by-youth, for-youth knowledge sharing creates space for young leaders to exchange information, ideas and hope, supporting a more diversified understanding of the nature-climate nexus

#Y4NKnowledgeSharing

CAPACITY-BUILDING

We aim to grow the capacity of youth to advocate for solutions rooted in nature and justice and be involved in their planning and implementation locally. Through global youth delegations and on-the-ground support and virtual skills-sharing, we help to equip young leaders with the skills and resources that they need to make change.

#Y4NCapacityBuilding

STORYTELLING

Through storytelling, we provide a platform for youth from all corners of the world to tell their stories about nature and climate and have their voices be heard. We amplify these stories digitally and together with the storytellers at high level decision making events, and we connect storytellers for peer-to-peer learning and support.

#Y4NStorytelling
